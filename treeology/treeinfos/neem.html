NEEM

Neem oil is a common pest repellant, effective against sand fleas
and mosquitoes. Other forms of neem can help control termites
and repel moths. Some manufacturers add neem to animal
shampoos to repel ticks and fleas. They may also add it to cattle
feed or grain to repel pests and parasites.Neem is a strong
antioxidant, neutralizing free radicals that may influence
the development of some conditions. It is also a
strong anti-inflammatory agent.
