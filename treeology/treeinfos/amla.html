AMLA

Amla, Amalaki or Indian Gooseberry is a highly potent medicinal plant
for its innumerable healing properties. Amalaki, as it
is popularly known in Sanskrit and in ancient
texts of Ayurveda, has various meanings
– mother, nurse, immortality and it also means ‘sour.’The small
green, pulpy, juicy fruit known as usirikaya in Telugu, Nellika in Tamil, goes with the botanical names Emblica Officinalis or Phyllanthus Emblica. Ayurveda strongly recommends eating a gooseberry daily for improving immunity, boosting metabolism and longevity.
