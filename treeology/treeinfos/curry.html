CURRY

Curry leaves are a part of a small deciduous aromatic shrub,
scientifically named Murraya koenigii, belonging to the
family Rutaceae. It may be considered a natural medicinal plant. South Asia
is home to this plant, and it is found in countries like Sri Lanka,
Bangladesh, China and India. In India,
it is found at the bottom of the
Himalayas, in Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Assam.
