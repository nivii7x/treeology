PEPPERMINT

The herb peppermint, a natural cross between two types of mint
(water mint and spearmint), grows throughout Europe and North
America. Both peppermint leaves and the essential oil from peppermint
have been used for health purposes. Peppermint oil
is the essential oil taken from the flowering parts and
leaves of the peppermint plant. (Essential oils are very
concentrated oils containing substances that give a
plant its characteristic odor or flavor.)
