ROSEWOOD

Rosewood is a deep, ruddy brown to purplish-brown colour, richly
streaked and grained with black resinous layers. It takes
a fine polish but because of its resinous nature is
difficult to work. The heartwood attains large dimensions, but squared
logs or planks are never seen because before the tree arrives
at maturity, the heartwood begins to decay, making it
faulty and hollow at the centre.
