THYME

Thyme (Thymus vulgaris) is an herb with a distinct smell.
The flowers, leaves, and oil are commonly used to flavor
foods and are also used as medicine. Thyme contains
chemicals that might help bacterial and fungal
infections. It also might help relieve coughing and have
antioxidant effects. People commonly use thyme for cough,
patchy hair loss (alopecia areata), dementia, and many other conditions,
but there is no good scientific evidence to support these uses.
